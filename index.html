About Me

Hello, I am Renato Francisco Souza de Oliveira, a professional passionate about technology and innovation. I have a degree in Physical Education, Digital Game Design, and I am studying Administration and DevOps. I have experience in software development, technical monitoring, and office assistance. I have knowledge in various programming languages, databases, computer networks, and computer assembly and maintenance. Basically, technology and innovation have been part of my life since I was a kid, when I tried to fix my video games by myself to adulthood where I sought to deepen my knowledge in programming courses. I am a curious, creative, proactive person committed to my goals. I like to learn new things, to work in a team, and to face challenges. In my free time, I like to be with my wife and son, play video games, learn something new, and practice sports.
